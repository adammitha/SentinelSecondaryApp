Sentinel Secondary is a comprehensive high school that currently enrolls over 1170 students in Grades 8 through 12. Sentinel also offers a number of special programs including Advanced Placement, French Immersion, Super Achievers and the Hockey, Soccer, and Tennis Academies.


School Value Statement:

At Sentinel Secondary School we:

  • Demonstrate a love of learning by modeling and encouraging personal excellence
  • Value integrity
  • Treat everyone with Dignity, Honesty, and Compassion
  • Behave in a collaborative and consistent manner
  • Are proud to be a safe, caring and inclusive community

School Motto:

Summis Cum Animis
(With Greatest Possible Spirit)
